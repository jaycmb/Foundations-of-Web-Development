Why we have to act NOW

Status Quo

The population of the planet is reaching unsustainable levels as it faces shortage of resources like water, fuel and food. But this has not stopped us from depleting earth resource. On August 1st, 2018, Human had already consumed more natural resources in 2018 than the Earth can regenerate that year. To put it in another way, humans are using natural resources 1.7 times faster than earth can generate. Rich countries are consuming 10 times more than poor countries, twice as much as the world average. Over two-thirds of our natural resources use, return into the atmosphere as Green House Gas emissions. Yet our demand on natural resource, is projected to double by 2050…

What we can do

What to do?

Realizing how critical the use of natural resources has an impact on planet earth, we envision a world, where human demand on natural resources use can be successfully reduced, through the introduction of eco-currency, innovative urban design, and eco-friendly lifestyle adoption. This can be achieved by implementing the principles of circular economy, aiming to use raw materials in the most efficient way.

Why it matters?

Consumers have never had so much clothing and at such dirt cheap prices. To be more precise, we have purchased 100% more items of clothing this year than we did just 30 years ago. We need to move the fashion industry away from the take-make-dispose consumption of clothes that are often produced in inhumane conditions and affect the environment in more damaging ways than most people realize

How to?

1

Avoid buying from Inditex, H&M and other fast fashion groups.

2

Support sustainable labels that work with recycled fabric and other reused materials

3

Buy less pieces but higher quality.
